# Chief Bogo’s Missing Donut

Chief Bogo was in his office. He looked very sad. His favorite donut was not on his desk! 

"Judy! Nick!" he shouted.

Judy Hopps and Nick Wilde ran into the office. 

"What happened, Chief?" Judy asked.

"My special strawberry donut is gone!" Bogo said.

Nick smiled. "Maybe someone was hungry."

They looked around the office. They saw crumbs on the floor. The crumbs led to the door.

"Follow the crumbs!" Judy said.

They followed the crumbs to the break room. They saw Clawhauser. He was eating a strawberry donut. He looked very happy.

"Clawhauser!" Judy said. "Is that the Chief’s donut?"

Clawhauser stopped eating. "Oh no! I found it on the desk. I thought it was a gift for everyone!"

Bogo walked into the room. He saw the empty donut box. He sighed.

"It is okay, Clawhauser," Bogo said. "But please ask me next time."

"I am sorry, Chief," Clawhauser said.

Nick laughed. "Don’t worry, Chief. I have an idea."

Nick went to the bakery. He bought ten strawberry donuts! 

"For everyone!" Nick said. 

Chief Bogo was happy again. Everyone in Zootopia shared the donuts. It was a great day. 

*Lesson: It is better to share with friends.*
